Principal's Message


I would like to thank everyone for one of the most interesting years I’ve had during my tenure of teaching. It was full of celebration and adversity, from championships to pandemics to success in the classroom and the challenges of teaching and learning from home. It was a year for the record books.


You as a graduating class were the first students at Hafford for a number of teachers. You were even my first class that I taught here. Visual Art 10, it was a great semester. After that semester I thought to myself that I had made the right decision and these were the best students and people around. Since then, you have not disappointed.


You are moving on to a new chapter in your lives. You have turned the page on this book of education and are thinking that you have hit the climax of your story. However, you will realize that you are still leading up the best part of this story in your lives. Whether you choose to further your education at a post secondary institution, jump straight into the trades, head into the work force, or travel. I’m positive you will land on you feet and run.

This may not be the graduation that you dreamed of, but it is the one that we’ve been dealt. We don’t always get what we want or deserve. But realize that this will be a story you can tell about the pandemic you lived through and conquered. These skills of adaptation and perseverance will only serve you as you move throughout your life…and hey, what a story it will make. In my closing words for you today, keep turning up the good!



Go Vikings and Viqueens!


Jamie Sommerfeld


Principal


Hafford Central School
